About Us

We deliver landmark projects

The company was established in the year 2008 as VarunKumar Constructions as Proprietorship concern, The company started functioning from small construction activities in various Residential projects,Road projects, Educational institutes and private sectors. Since then matching its pace with time it has metamorphosed into one of the respected engineering, construction companies in Bangalore, Karnataka serving both private and public sectors. VKC has been actively participating in the high growth opportunities offered by Indian infrastructure Industry, more specifically in the construction sector. VKC has always responded to the challenge by taking an active role in projects of national importance in diverse areas ranging from Residential’s, Educational Institute, Pharmaceutical Industries and Roads. VKC is firmly committed to its quality motto of “We Deliver” On Time within Budget with commitment.
